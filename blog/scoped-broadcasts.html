Introducing Scoped Broadcasts: Reach the Right People, Instantly

For too long, sports event organisers have been stuck in a communication nightmare. You need to reach the coaches at your event — but not all of them. Maybe it's just the U14 teams, or only the clubs from a specific region, or every coach registered for Saturday's fixtures.

Until now, that meant juggling multiple WhatsApp groups, sending the same message three times, and hoping nobody got missed. Today, we're changing that with Scoped Broadcasts.

What Are Scoped Broadcasts?

Scoped Broadcasts let you target your messages with precision. Instead of blasting every coach in your database, you choose exactly who should receive each message based on three scoping levels:

  • Event scope — reach every coach registered for a specific event
  • Club scope — message all coaches affiliated with particular clubs across your events
  • Team scope — target coaches of specific teams within an event

The result? Zero noise. Every coach gets exactly what's relevant to them, and nothing else.

Why This Matters

We spoke to dozens of organisers before building this feature. The same pain points came up again and again:

I manage 200+ teams across four events a month. I was spending more time figuring out who to message than actually writing the messages.

Scoped Broadcasts solve this by making the "who" automatic. Select your scope, write your message, and hit send. Ludum handles the rest — including deduplication, so a coach who belongs to multiple clubs won't get spammed.

How It Works

From your Ludum dashboard, create a new broadcast and choose your scope. You'll see a live preview of exactly how many coaches will receive your message before you send it. No guesswork, no duplicates, no missed coaches.

Every broadcast includes read receipts, so you'll know exactly who's seen your message and who might need a follow-up.

Getting Started

Scoped Broadcasts are available today for all Ludum Events organisers. Log into your dashboard to try it out, or book a demo if you're new to the platform.
